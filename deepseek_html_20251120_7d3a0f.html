Standard Checkers Rules

  • Move your pieces (red) diagonally forward
  • Capture opponent pieces by jumping over them
  • If a capture is available, you must take it
  • Pieces become Kings when they reach the opposite side
  • Kings can move and capture diagonally in any direction
  • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or blocking their moves
